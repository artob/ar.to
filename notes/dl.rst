Hardware
========

`GPUs <gpu>`__
--------------

- **Recommended**: NVIDIA GTX 1080 Ti, GTX 1080, GTX 1070

- **On a small budget**: NVIDIA GTX 1060 (6 GB)

- **On a tiny budget**: NVIDIA GTX 1050 Ti (4 GB)
